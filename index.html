Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. It is Hazzam's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you through our app, Lingo.

This app does not need any personal information of users to provide a service. We collect data by fair and lawful means, with your knowledge and consent. We also let you know why we’re collecting it and how it will be used.

The app uses storage permissions, which allows the app to extend its functionality once the user has allowed the app to access storage. There are two tasks regarding storage permission in this app. One is to select an image from the gallery, and the other one is to save a PDF version of your vocabularies in the storage.

What data we store, we’ll protect within commercially acceptable means to prevent loss and theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use or modification.

Our app may link to external sites that are not operated by us. Please be aware that we have no control over the content and practices of these sites, and cannot accept responsibility or liability for their respective privacy policies.

This app also allows you to search for meanings and images of a vocabulary from several websites. These websites are:

  • Google Translate
  • Mictosoft Translator
  • Yandex Translator

Your continued use of our app will be regarded as acceptance of our practices around privacy and personal information. If you have any questions about how we handle user data and personal information, feel free to contact us.
